Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about connecting with businesses and placing orders.

1. How does Fmk Link help customers and businesses connect?

Fmk Link provides a platform where customers can easily place orders from a variety of businesses listed together. After placing an order, businesses can directly communicate with customers through email, ensuring a smooth and personal interaction.

2. Can businesses customize their profiles on Fmk Link?

Yes, businesses can create detailed profiles showcasing their services or products. This helps customers make informed decisions when selecting the right business for their needs.

3. How can I place an order on Fmk Link?

Placing an order is simple: browse through the listings of businesses and select the products or services you need. After selecting your items, you can proceed with placing the order, and the business will contact you directly via email for further communication.

4. How do I make payment for my order?

Currently, payments are handled in person, and customers can pay directly to the business once the order is confirmed. Businesses will provide details on how and where to complete the payment.

5. How can I contact customer support?

If you have any questions or issues regarding your order, you can reach out to customer support via email at fmklink@gmail.com. You can also contact us by phone at 0767525234 or 0658625234. We are available to assist you with any inquiries you may have.

6. How can businesses contact their customers?

Businesses can communicate directly with customers via Gmail. After an order is placed, the business will reach out through email for order confirmation, delivery details, or any other necessary communication.
