Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions our customers have.

1. What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 30 days of purchase. Items must be unused and in their original packaging. Please contact our support team for assistance with your return.

2. Do you offer free shipping?

Yes, we offer free standard shipping on orders over $50. For orders below that, a shipping fee will apply. Expedited shipping options are also available at an additional cost.

3. How can I track my order?

Once your order has shipped, you will receive an email with a tracking number and a link to track your order on the courier's website. You can also track your order through your account page.

4. Can I cancel or modify my order?

Orders can be modified or canceled within 1 hour of purchase. After this period, we are unable to make changes as the order will already be processed for shipment.

5. What payment methods do you accept?

We accept a variety of payment methods including major credit/debit cards, PayPal, and Apple Pay. For more options, please refer to our payment page.

6. How do I contact customer support?

You can reach our customer support team via email at support@ourshop.com or by calling (123) 456-7890. We are available from 9 AM to 6 PM, Monday to Friday.
